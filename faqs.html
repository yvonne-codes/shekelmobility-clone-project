What is Shekel Trade?
Shekel Trade is a B2B marketplace that allows car dealers to buy and sell cars on the Shekel Mobility platform
How does Shekel Trade work?
Car dealers can create an account on Shekel Trade and list their cars for sale. Buyers can browse the marketplace and contact dealers to inquire about cars. Once a buyer and seller agree on a price, they can complete the transaction through Shekel Trade.
What are the benefits of using Shekel Trade?
Shekel Trade offers a number of benefits to car dealers, including: ⁠Access to a large marketplace of buyers and sellers, ⁠A secure and efficient platform for completing transactions, ⁠A variety of tools and resources to help dealers manage their inventory and sales
Is Shekel Trade free?
No, Shekel Trade is not free. Listing cars is free, but buying and selling cars requires a paid subscription. We have a variety of subscription options to choose from.
How do I get started with Shekel Trade?
To get started with Shekel Trade, simply create an account on the Shekel Mobility website or app. Once your account is created, you can start listing cars for sale or browsing the marketplace for cars to buy.
