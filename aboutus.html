ABOUT SHEKEL MOBILITY

We are a B2B marketplace and manage system for auto dealers.

At Shekel Mobility, our goal is to help auto dealerships realize their full market potential by providing them with the tools and resources they require to grow sustainably and optimally.

We empower these businesses by providing financial and management services like credit facilities to dealers, enabling secure cross-border transactions and connections, automating business operations, while ensuring an ecosystem built with end-to-end security.

OUR VISION

To create the world's largest and most inclusive auto dealership ecosystem in Africa and beyond, powered by technology.

OUR MISSION

Building a simpler, faster, and smarter solution for auto dealerships across emerging markets, to create room for opportunity growth within an industry where access is limited
