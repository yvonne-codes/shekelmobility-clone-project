Hello Incoming Sheyk!

Here is our guide to joining one of the fastest-growing teams ever!

Our culture reflects who we are and how we interact with each other, as well as with everyone else outside the team. At Shekel Mobility, we identify as the “Sheyks,” a cool name if we do say so ourselves.

Sheyk is coined from the word, “Sheikh”, which means an Arab Leader, particularly the chief of an Arab tribe, family or village.

At Shekel Mobility, We
identify as the “Sheyks”

As a result, Sheyks are characterized
by our exemplary leadership qualities,
both as individuals and in the industry.

We believe in everyone on the team and
trust each other to spread the word
about Shekel Mobility wherever we find
ourselves.

How many puns can you come up with
using the word, “Sheyk?” Here is one. We
hope that if you do join us, you get to
really sheyk things up around here!
